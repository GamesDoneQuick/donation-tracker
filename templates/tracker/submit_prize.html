Notes For Taking Prize Images

Try and take good photos, especially of your own work! Be proud of it! Some basic photography tips for ANY camera:
  • Use lots of lights and turn off the camera's flash. A well-lit room (or especially outdoor sun) will provide a much better image overall, especially for smartphone pictures. If you MUST use flash, avoid glare.
  • Take many photos! You can always delete the bad ones later.
  • Orient your camera/phone to fit the subject. If the object is really tall, shoot your photo in portrait (tall). If it's wide, shoot it in landscape (wide). A common mistake for smartphone users is to take everything in portrait, resulting in tiny photos.
  • Don't upload to Facebook or other social media pages for this submission. They dramatically reduce the quality of your image.
